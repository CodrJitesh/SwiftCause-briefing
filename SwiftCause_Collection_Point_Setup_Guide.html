Set Up Your Collection Point

Turn any phone or tablet into a contactless donation station for your charity. No special hardware required.

Takes about 10 minutes

SwiftCause works on any device with a web browser. This guide walks you through choosing a device, securing it, and placing it where donors will see it. You can set up multiple collection points at no extra cost.

3

Lock the device to SwiftCause

Prevent visitors from closing the donation page or accessing other apps. This is free and takes 2 minutes.

Samsung Knox Kiosk Mode (Free, built-in)

  1. Open Settings \u2192 Biometrics and security \u2192 Other security settings
  2. Tap Pin windows and toggle it ON
  3. Open Chrome and go to your SwiftCause donation page URL
  4. Tap the Recent apps button (square icon)
  5. Tap the app icon at the top of the Chrome card \u2192 select Pin this app
  6. The device is now locked to your donation page. To unpin, press and hold Back + Recent together.

Android Screen Pinning (Free, built-in)

  1. Open Settings \u2192 Security \u2192 Advanced \u2192 App pinning
  2. Toggle it ON. Optionally set \u201cAsk for PIN before unpinning\u201d
  3. Open Chrome and go to your SwiftCause donation page URL
  4. Tap the Recent apps button \u2192 tap the pin icon at the bottom of the Chrome card
  5. Done! The device is locked to SwiftCause. To unpin, hold Back + Recent (or enter your PIN).

Apple Guided Access (Free, built-in)

  1. Open Settings \u2192 Accessibility \u2192 Guided Access
  2. Toggle Guided Access ON
  3. Tap Passcode Settings and set a 6-digit passcode
  4. Open Safari and go to your SwiftCause donation page URL
  5. Triple-click the side button (or Home button on older iPads)
  6. Tap Start in the top-right corner
  7. The device is now locked to SwiftCause. To exit, triple-click again and enter your passcode.
4

Plug in and place it

Keep your device charged and visible.

\ud83d\udea2

Near the entrance or exit

Donors are most likely to give when arriving or leaving.

\u26a1

Always plugged in

Use a long charging cable routed through the stand.

\ud83d\udca1

Well-lit area

Make sure the screen is visible. Turn brightness to maximum.

\ud83d\udcac

Add a sign

A small sign saying \u201cTap to donate\u201d makes a big difference.

Do

  • Set screen brightness to maximum
  • Turn off auto-sleep
  • Connect to reliable Wi-Fi
  • Route charging cable neatly
  • Test a real donation first

Don't

  • Leave on battery power overnight
  • Place in direct sunlight
  • Use weak or guest Wi-Fi
  • Forget to lock the device
  • Hide it in a corner
